About us

Welcome to
VICTORY INTERNATIONAL


We are a company dedicated to providing high-quality clothing for men, women, and children. We are designed with style, comfort, and durability in mind, ensuring that you look and feel your best. We primarily work with high-fashion buyers who demand more products in blends, such as bamboo and cotton, bamboo and elastane; cotton, polyester, viscose with elastane, to name a few. Working with our vendors on a regular basis, the company keeps assessing customers and places orders with those vendors who can produce high-fashion fabric and nice finishing in a given timeframe. The company offers a complete range of woven and knitted garments for Men, Women, Girls, Boys, and kids. We are proud to offer competitive pricing for Victory International products, making it easier than ever to look your best without breaking the bank.

Overseas Production

We've partnered with certified factories in Poland, UK, and US . This strategic collaboration ensures adherence to quality standards and optimizes production efficiency. By leveraging these global resources, we enhance our market position and mitigate supply chain risks.

Joint Venture

Partnering with Prime Global Industries in Tirupur, our joint venture can produce 25,000 hi-fashion T-shirts monthly. Emphasizing sample development, we prioritize prompt delivery and effective crisis management.
